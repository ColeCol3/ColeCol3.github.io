Flappy die

My final project is called flappy die. It's like flappy bird but instead you are trying to shoot the bird.

"The bird will fly out in random directions, and your goal is to click on it to shoot. You have 3 lives, and if the bird falls without being shot 3 times, the game is over.

I think the highlights of my projects are the hearts, The code for the hearts combines visuals (costumes), logic (conditions), real-time updates (forever loop), and interactivity (events and sound).
